This game does not take any actual input but rather it's a story that's being told. The player must venture through various obstacles with the goal of finding his home.

You will fight monsters, seek assistance, gain levels, find new weapons in order to help you on your journey.

Please open Developers console.

  • Chrome: CTRL + SHIFT + J
  • Firefox: CTRL + SHIFT + I
  • The objective is to reach your house.
  • You must be at least level 5.
  • Level 5 can be obtained by fighting enouh monsters. You gain 1 leve with each battle.

Press F5 to restart the game.
